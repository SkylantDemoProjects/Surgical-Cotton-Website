Blood Collection Tubes

Available Sizes: 4 ml

Packaging: 100 pcs boxes

Our Blood Collection Tubes are high-quality, sterile tubes designed for safe, accurate, and hygienic blood collection in hospitals, laboratories, and diagnostic centers. Available in different types and additives, they ensure the stability and integrity of collected blood samples for reliable testing and analysis.

Available Types

  • Plain Tubes (Red Top): No additives, used for serum collection.
  • EDTA Tubes (Lavender Top): Contains EDTA as anticoagulant for hematology tests.
  • Sodium Citrate Tubes (Blue Top): Contains sodium citrate for coagulation studies.
  • Heparin Tubes (Green Top): For plasma determinations and biochemical tests.
  • Fluoride Tubes (Gray Top): Contains sodium fluoride for glucose testing.

Features

  • Sterile, single-use, and safe
  • Transparent for easy sample visibility
  • Available with different additives for various tests
  • Leak-proof and strong for transport and storage
  • Compatible with automated analyzers and centrifuges
  • Standardized sizes: 2 ml, 3 ml, 5 ml, 7 ml, 10 ml

Uses

  • Blood sample collection for laboratory testing
  • Hematology, biochemistry, and serology tests
  • Clinical diagnostics and research applications
  • Safe transport and storage of blood samples
  • Hospital and medical center usage
