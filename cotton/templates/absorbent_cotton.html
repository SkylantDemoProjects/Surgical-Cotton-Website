Absorbent Cotton

Available Range: 20 gm | 50 gm | 100 gm | 200 gm | 500 gm

Absorbent Cotton is a soft, fibrous material made from 100% natural cotton, thoroughly cleaned and bleached to ensure high absorbency and purity. It is sterile and safe, making it ideal for medical, surgical, and first-aid applications. Absorbent cotton is widely used in hospitals, clinics, and personal care for wound dressing, cleaning, and hygiene purposes.

Features

  • 100% Pure Cotton
  • Highly Absorbent
  • Soft and Gentle
  • Sterile and Safe

Uses

  • Wound care: Cleaning and dressing wounds
  • Medical and surgical: Used in hospitals and clinics for procedures and dressing
  • First-aid kits: Essential for emergency care
  • Personal hygiene: Skin cleaning, baby care, and beauty applications
  • Laboratory use: Absorbent material for sample handling
