Gauze Cloth Than

Available Range:100 gm | 300 gm | 400 gm | 500 gm

Gauze Cloth Than is a high-quality, bleached cotton fabric known for its softness, breathability, and excellent absorbency. It is widely used in hospitals and clinics for dressing wounds, bandaging, and making surgical dressings.

Features

  • Made from 100% pure cotton for superior softness and absorbency.
  • Available in bleached and unbleached forms, with uniform weaving.
  • Highly absorbent and gentle on the skin
  • Highly breathable and lint-free, ensuring comfort and hygiene.
  • Durable fabric suitable for sterilization and medical applications.

Uses

  • Widely used in hospitals for dressing wounds and surgical procedures.
  • Ideal for making bandages, swabs, and dressings.
  • First-aid kits: Essential for emergency care
  • Suitable for industrial, cosmetic, and personal hygiene purposes.
  • Also used in laboratories and clinics for cleaning and sterilization tasks.
